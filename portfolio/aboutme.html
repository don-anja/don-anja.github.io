About Me

Hi there! My name is Anja and I'm from Zagreb, Croatia. I'm a passionate web developer who loves dogs, listening to Queen, and diving into the magical world of The Lord of the Rings.

I made the exciting switch from working with people to web development because I found my true passion in bringing ideas to life through coding. I enjoy the challenges and the creativity that come with web development, and I'm always eager to learn and explore new technologies.

When I'm not coding, you can find me exploring the great outdoors with my furry friends, or curled up with a good book and a cup of tea.
